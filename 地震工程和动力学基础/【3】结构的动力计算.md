## 单自由度体系的自由振动
{: id="20210308195426-8wassux" updated="20210308195442"}

**自由振动**：单自由度体系初始时受外界干扰起振，此后再无外界干扰。可以是初位移，也可以是初速度。
{: id="20210308201910-0svzc65" updated="20210308202010"}

$$
m\ddot{y}(t)+ky(t)=0
$$
{: id="20210308202412-oqb7ifv" updated="20210308202712"}

$$
y(t)=asin(\omega t+\alpha)\qquad \alpha=\sqrt{y_0^2+(\frac{v_0}{\omega})^2} \qquad tg\alpha=\frac{y_0\omega}{v_0}
$$
{: id="20210308202708-0tayl11" updated="20210308203351"}

$$
T=\frac{2\pi}{\omega}=2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}=2\pi\sqrt{m\delta}=2\pi\sqrt{\frac{W\delta}{g}}=2\pi\sqrt{\frac{\Delta_{st}}{g}}
$$
{: id="20210308202713-2dh1tr6" updated="20210308204342"}

### 简谐荷载
{: id="20210315192124-0l06n30" updated="20210315192216"}

$$
F_p(t)=Fsin(\theta t)
$$
{: id="20210315192219-qtmserm" updated="20210315192245"}

#### 等效静态位移
{: id="20210315192221-wnysnzy" updated="20210315192542"}

$$
y_{st}=\frac{F}{m\omega^2}=\frac{F}{k}=F\delta
$$
{: id="20210315192542-uop9od3" updated="20210315192618"}

- {: id="20210315192627-gym0zfj"}$y_{st}$为动荷载幅值F产生的静位移
  {: id="20210315192627-svhu8kx" updated="20210315192700"}
{: id="20210315192546-f820x1s" updated="20210315192626"}

$$
y(t)=y_{st}\frac{1}{1-\frac{\theta^2}{\omega^2}}(sin(\theta t)-\frac{\theta}{\omega}sin(\omega t))
$$
{: id="20210315192911-j9uv70e" updated="20210315193110"}

$$
\beta=\frac{1}{1-\frac{\theta^2}{\omega^2}}
$$
{: id="20210315192129-fyhksni" updated="20210315193636"}

- {: id="20210315193641-8lxmnlm"}$\beta$为动力系数
  {: id="20210315193641-okxjo4h" updated="20210315193702"}
{: id="20210315193614-4mrdwty" updated="20210315193641"}

#### 讨论$\beta$的变化规律
{: id="20210315193727-ebrufo4" updated="20210315193743"}

1. {: id="20210315193748-4zly1q2"}$\theta << \omega$, $\theta/\omega\rightarrow0$.
   {: id="20210315193748-kkq462u" updated="20210315193846"}

   - {: id="20210315193851-kpwvy9j"}此时，$\beta\rightarrow1$，振幅几乎不变
     {: id="20210315193851-o4zvsnv" updated="20210315193925"}
   - {: id="20210315193927-18f3x03"}此时，动荷载类似于静荷载
     {: id="20210315193927-4gknb7k" updated="20210315193936"}
   {: id="20210315193848-8rv378h" updated="20210315193851"}
2. {: id="20210315193957-6pxr7lj"}$\theta\rightarrow\omega$，即$\theta/\omega\rightarrow1$.
   {: id="20210315193957-hd4u1pz" updated="20210315194028"}

   - {: id="20210315194315-2e4qcez"}理论上会达到无穷大
     {: id="20210315194315-g0pke4e" updated="20210315194322"}
   {: id="20210315194311-t9s7m1f" updated="20210315194315"}
3. {: id="20210315194131-1b7vw6a"}$\theta>>\omega$，即$\theta/\omega>>1$.
   {: id="20210315194131-m7xw8pz" updated="20210315194157"}

   - {: id="20210315194238-6ptkpjd"}此时最大幅值逐步减小，趋近于0
     {: id="20210315194238-dvd9pfg" updated="20210315194307"}
   {: id="20210315194237-kn98ml3" updated="20210315194238"}
{: id="20210315193743-jyqwshn" updated="20210315194142"}

### 刚度法
{: id="20210322191935-apram4v" updated="20210322191944"}

$$
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
m_1\ddot{y_1}(t)+r_1&=0 \\
m_2\ddot{y_2}(t)+r_2&=0
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
{: id="20210322191945-qekhnqb" updated="20210322192242"}

$$
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
r_1&=k_{11}y_1(t)+k_{12}y_2(t) \\
r_2&=k_{21}y_1(t)+k_{22}y_2(t)
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
{: id="20210322192302-8goj74u" updated="20210322192344"}

$$
\left[
\begin{array}{rcl}
m_1&0\\
0&m_2
\end{array} 
\right]
\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}
\ddot{y}_1\\
\ddot{y}_2
\end{array}
\right\}+
\left[
\begin{array}{rcl}
k_{11}&k_{12}\\
k_{21}&k_{22}
\end{array}
\right]
\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}
y_1\\
y_2
\end{array}
\right\}=
$$
{: id="20210322192409-80rg0cr" updated="20210322192829"}

$$
[M]\{\ddot{y}\}+[K]\{y\}=\{0\}
